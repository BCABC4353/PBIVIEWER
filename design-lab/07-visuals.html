V7 — Waterfall: the directional ruling, with the pre-ruling alternate for comparison

PBI: waterfall chart, in the owner's ruling: “Safety Orange up. Blue down. Logo colors.” Increments pour in orange, decrements in backlit blue, totals hold silver, connectors stay hairline. The pair is arithmetic, not judgment — payments shrinking A/R is good news wearing blue — and amber stays reserved for genuinely overdue dollars.
