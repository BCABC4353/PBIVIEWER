12 — The app: full product screens
Every prior board assembled into one product. Seven screens in phone frames, sharing one grammar: freshness always top-right, status always glyph + label left of its timestamp, one lit element per screen, categorical hues for data only, chrome monochrome and silent. Below: the DENIALS screen under weather, and the fluid law proven at three widths.
