SHARED-ELEMENT MORPH — GEOMETRY CONTINUOUS, CONTENT CROSS-FADES
Tap a row — it grows into the detail sheet; the same title, the same tape, the same age figure travel there. Tap the header or drag down to shrink it back. Tap mid-flight and it reverses from exactly where it is, velocity carried — the spring never restarts.
The medium tape cross-fades into the calibrated large tape inside one continuously-scaling frame. Detail rows surface on 25 ms staggers only once the geometry has settled — data may animate its arrival; the sheet itself is chrome and performs nothing beyond the morph the finger asked for.
